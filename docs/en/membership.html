Dorval Horticultural & Ecological Society

Our purpose

The object of the Society shall be to improve and develop the horticultural and ecological knowledge of its members, thereby contributing to the health and beauty of our environment.

Read our constitution or download our brochure to learn more about the DHES.

MEMBERSHIP

Join our community


Benefits of Membership

  • Monthly meetings with guest speakers
  • Horticultural workshops
  • Local garden tours
  • Discounts at some Quebec nurseries

Other activities include

  • Annual perennial sale
  • Bus tours to exceptional gardens
  • Social events with a floral theme

Cost of Membership

Annual (January to December) membership per family (living at the same address) is: $15 for Dorval residents or $20 for non-residents.
Choose one of the following options to join or renew your membership.

  • 1 ONLINE: Complete the membership form, save it and attach it to an e-mail addressed to dorvalhort@gmail.com, and pay via Interac e-transfer to dorvalhort@gmail.com. Be sure to put your full name and the word "membership" in the comments/notes section of the transfer.

  • 2 BY MAIL: Print the completed form and mail it with your cheque (payable to DHES) to:

    Dorval Horticultural & Ecological Society (DHES)
    1335 ch. Bord-du-Lac,
    Dorval, QC H9S 2E5

  • 3 IN PERSON: Come to any meeting, register, and pay (cash or cheque payable to DHES).

Pick up your membership card at any meeting.
